How does it work?

  • Delegate

    Delegate your ADA with to pool HEN. Your delegation will be registered on Cardano. We will use that information when we the pool mint its first block.

  • Transfer ADA

    Transfer 2 ADA to this address: ABCD. Transfering a token to your address requires a small amount of ADA including fees. We will use this ADA when sending your NFT.

  • Help us

    It takes time for a pool to get selected to mint block by the Cardano network. Sit back and relax. You can help us by sharing our pool HEN with your social networks to help speed up the process.

  • First minted block

    Once the pool mints its first block, we will reward all our delegators who have sent the initial 2 ADA to ABCD with a NFT from this collection.
